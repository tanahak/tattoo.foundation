check a carrier before you sign on

Free background check on any US trucking company. Type a USDOT number or company name and see their operating authority, insurance on file, crash record, roadside inspection history, and safety scores — the same data brokers and big fleets pay for. If a recruiter's story doesn't match what you see here, walk away.
