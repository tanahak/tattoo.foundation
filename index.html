human trafficking's secret role in trucking

Trafficking survivors, foster kids aging out of care, and other vulnerable populations are deliberately fed to trucking's high-turnover industry. Because they're already conditioned to accept abuse, illegal carriers exploit them through complex labor trafficking schemes.

These survivors become perfect victims who won't resist, won't report, and won't run. We break this predatory pipeline with:

  • Grants For Employment Lawyers
  • Temporary Housing To Find Legal Employment
  • Career Re-Training
  • Training To Identify Labor Trafficking
  • Safelist- Helps Drivers Avoid Illegal Employers

the human trafficking to trucking pipeline

Every day, human trafficking survivors are rebranded as truck drivers with promises of a new life. What they often find is that the only change is who is doing the pimping.

Through the "Biden Harris Trucking Plan," the government actively targets international survivors of human trafficking to be brought here and trained as truckers. They also target other marginalized communities including the homeless and foster children aging out of care - populations already vulnerable to exploitation.

Labor traffickers quickly identify survivors as prime targets for trafficking because, after all, they are used to the systems of control. Illegal carriers take their playbook from sex trafficking models, and retraumatize survivors within a system that promised to rescue them.

"I remember spending days and days alone in a hotel room 'in-call', now I spend months on end alone in a truck traveling the highways. The truck becomes the new cage. The carrier becomes the new pimp. The brokers and shippers are the clients. The debt-bondage, the fear, and the threats remain the same - just wrapped in the respectability of 'honest work.'"

Government policy inadvertently feeds vulnerable people into a system designed to exploit them, all while legitimate carriers struggle to compete against illegal operators using trafficked labor.

breaking their weapons of control

Illegal carriers create unfair marketplace conditions that hurt legitimate carriers. We systematically remove their competitive advantages.

  • Illegal carriers ignore Hours of Service laws, running solo drivers 24/7 like teams
  • They commit systematic wage theft to underbid legitimate freight rates
  • Housing insecurity keeps quality drivers trapped with exploitative employers
  • False pay benchmarks from illegal schedules distort driver expectations
  • Legitimate carriers lose experienced drivers to exploitation-based retention
  • Regulatory scrutiny increases industry-wide due to bad actors

Strategic Partnership Opportunity

By funding our work, legitimate carriers invest in removing illegal competitors while gaining access to rescued drivers as experienced hires.

Result: Fair competition based on merit, not exploitation.

Shifting Gears

Trauma-informed tech reskilling program for drivers who've experienced labor trafficking, wage theft, or career-ending barriers like medical disqualification.

  • 6-month coding bootcamp with guaranteed employment
  • Web development, AI, and automation training
  • Trauma-informed learning environment
  • Direct employment with T.A.T.T.O.O. Foundation
  • $65K average starting salary

Safe Haven

Emergency and long-term housing for drivers who lose jobs and homes when fired or disqualified, breaking the housing trap that keeps drivers with illegal carriers.

  • 24/7 emergency housing assistance
  • Hotel accommodations for displaced drivers
  • Transitional housing during skills training
  • Dedicated residential facility planned
  • Safe extraction from exploitative situations
