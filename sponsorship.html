501(c)(3) nonprofit • survivor-founded • AI informed

building pathways out

We create sustainable transition programs for drivers escaping exploitation. Through housing, education, and career retraining, we're building the infrastructure that gives people real choices.

shared interests

While our mission focuses on driver welfare, we recognize that the same illegal operators exploiting drivers also create unfair market conditions for legitimate businesses.

marketplace stability

By helping drivers escape exploitative situations, we reduce the workforce available to illegal operators, naturally correcting market distortions.

quality hiring pipeline

Drivers who complete our programs bring experience, stability, and an understanding of what legitimate employment looks like.

industry reputation

Supporting driver welfare initiatives demonstrates commitment to ethical business practices and industry leadership.

regulatory alignment

Our work supports compliance and safety initiatives that benefit all legitimate operators in the long term.

cost reduction

Lower turnover and training expenses through access to experienced, stable drivers who understand professional standards.

risk mitigation

Enhanced compliance and safety standards that reduce liability exposure and strengthen operational integrity.

resource not rescue

We provide tools that empower people to build their own sustainable futures

skills transition

Technology bootcamps and career training for drivers seeking alternatives to over-the-road work

legal support

Employment lawyers helping drivers recover stolen wages and escape debt bondage situations

🗃️

carrier intelligence

AI-native carrier verification — FMCSA authority history, insurance filings, H-2B labor signals — helping drivers identify legitimate employers and avoid exploitation. Published at mcp.tattoo.foundation and data.supertrucker.ai.

🤝

peer mentorship

Driver-to-driver support networks connecting experienced professionals with those navigating industry challenges

💰

financial assistance

Emergency funds and microgrants providing immediate relief during transition periods and crisis situations
