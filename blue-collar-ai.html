why this is your moment

Most small carriers can't afford to invest in AI. That hesitation is the opening. Carriers who get integrated now will be the ones agentic marketplaces route loads to first.

incumbents are stalling

The mid-market carriers running 50–500 trucks are mostly waiting to "see how AI shakes out." They're the ones you compete with — and they're standing still.

agentic marketplaces are real

Freight is moving toward AI-to-AI booking. Brokers' agents talk to carriers' agents, negotiate rates, and book loads. If your dispatch isn't reachable by another agent, you're invisible.

small fleets can move first

You don't have a six-month procurement committee. You have a phone and a decision-maker. That's the actual advantage — not capital, speed.

AI-native posture matters

Shippers and brokers are starting to filter for "AI-ready" carriers. Being on the list early is worth more than being on it perfectly.

what the grant covers

Built and integrated for you. Not a course, not a course-corrected SaaS bill — a working system you own.
