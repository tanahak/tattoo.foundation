Richard Walker sues Federal Express Corporation

Walker alleges Fair Labor Standards Act violations against Federal Express Corporation

2026-05-22 · source: www.courtlistener.com

RICHARD WALKER has filed a lawsuit against FEDERAL EXPRESS CORPORATION, successor by merger to FedEx Ground Package System, Inc., alleging Fair Labor Standards Act violations. The complaint, filed on May 22, 2026, in the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Wisconsin, demands a jury trial.

Case Details

The case, Walker v. Federal Express Corporation, is assigned to Judge William Duffin. The plaintiff, Richard Walker, is represented by attorney Shannon Liss-Riordan of the firm Lichten & Liss-Riordan. The defendant, Federal Express Corporation, has not yet responded to the complaint.

Complaint Allegations

The complaint alleges that Federal Express Corporation violated the Fair Labor Standards Act, which is a federal law that sets standards for minimum wage, overtime pay, and other employment practices. The complaint does not specify the exact nature of the alleged violations, but it does demand a jury trial and seeks unspecified damages.
